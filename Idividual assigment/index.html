What is an escape room:

Escape Games are adventures where players solve a series of riddles and puzzles using team work, observation, creative and logical thinking as well as teamwork. Participants are given a set time limit to accomplish the mission or escape. Games are set in a variety of fictional locations, such as train, a museum heist, a time travel machine and include the various puzzles and riddles of said theme.

Having built over 8 escape games, we at Conundroom, understand that a successful game should involve a full team, include a challenge and be memorable and fun.

Frequently Asked Questions:
Can I bring more people along even though I already registered?
Absolutely! Although an extra payment of $31 per person would have to be made when the team gets here.
Is there anything scary inside?
Absolutely not, we are very family friendly, thus our minimal age limit is set to 13 for adult quests and 7 for kids quests.
We have over 10 people in our team, can you accommodate?
Yes we can! We are now have 3 quests that are next to each other. Each quest can have up to 10 people playing at a time. When booking make sure you book three rooms at the same time!
Is it wheelchair accessible?
All of our quests are fully wheelchair accessible.
can i bring a child or baby under 7 years old?
No problem! Kids under 7 can definitely accompany adult teams into the game for free. We can host at most two kids for free in some of our games.
cancelation policy
Our cancelation policy: no cancellation fee applies for cancellations made 24 or more hours in advance. A cancellation fee of $50 applies for cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance. No refund applies in case of no-show.
